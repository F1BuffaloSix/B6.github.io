About F1 in Schools

F1 in Schools is an international competition where high school students work together applying math, science, technology, engineering and design skills to design, build and race miniature Formula 1 cars.

The cars are tested on a 20-meter track to measure speed, with awards going to the fastest and best engineered designs. Through F1 in Schools, students gain hands-on engineering experience as they design, manufacture, test and race model F1 cars.
