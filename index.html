Buffalo Six Racing Team

Mexico's Premier F1 in Schools Competitors

Buffalo Six in F1 Schools Competition

Buffalo Six (also known as B6) is a competitive student team participating in F1 in Schools, an international competition where high school students apply mathematics, science, technology, engineering and design skills to create and race miniature Formula 1 cars.

Our team develops CO2-powered cars tested on a 20-meter track for maximum speed and engineering excellence. Through F1 in Schools, Buffalo Six team members gain hands-on engineering experience while designing, manufacturing, testing and racing model F1 cars representing Mexico in international competitions.
