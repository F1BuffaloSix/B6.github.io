Mission

Becoming leaders in the F1 in Schools competition by designing and manufacturing the most aerodynamic and efficient car, driven by our passion for STEM and business. Through teamwork, research, and continuous improvement, we will strive to win the national championship to access the professional category.

Vision

Learning new things through our participation in F1 in Schools, using our effort and dedication to win and be recognized in this competition. Inspiring other teams by demonstrating excellence in design, racing, and innovative business plans, while acquiring valuable knowledge in various areas such as engineering, aerodynamics, and entrepreneurship.
